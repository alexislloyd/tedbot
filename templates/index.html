About TIM

TIM is a platform for ideas worth automating. TIM generates talks on a broad spectrum of topics, based on the texts of slightly more coherent talks given under the auspices of his more famous big brother, who shall not be named here. You can keep up-to-date on newly generated talks on Twitter at @bottalks.
